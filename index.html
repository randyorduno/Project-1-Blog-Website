Guillermo Ochoa Was Unstoppable


I would consider this goalkeeper as one of the Top 10 during these few last games. Many may assume that he hasn't done a great job latley but these previous games reveal otherwise. During the game of Monterrey vs Club America on Dec 26th, the rival team, Monterrey had 20 shots but only 6 of them were shots with a possible goal opportunity but Ochoa saved 4 out of those 6. Clearly the score could have been much worse for Club America. So even if they didn't win, it should be clrear for every one that we need to give credit to the man who did everything in their power to actually try and win the game.
